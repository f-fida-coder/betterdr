A) How to play - top

Overview

Blackjack is a popular card game where you compete against the dealer. Each hand aims to reach a total as close to 21 as possible without going over. Face cards count as 10, Aces count as 1 or 11, and all other cards count at face value.

Objective

Beat the dealer by: (1) having a higher total than the dealer without busting, or (2) letting the dealer bust (exceed 21). A two‑card 21 is a Blackjack.

How to Play

  1. Place Your Main Bet: Choose your stake and confirm the bet.
  2. Initial Deal: You receive two cards face up; the dealer receives one face up (the up‑card) and one face down (the hole card).
  3. Your Turn: Choose actions (Hit, Stand, Double, Split, Surrender where allowed). See detailed actions below.
  4. Dealer Turn: After all player hands complete, the dealer reveals the hole card and draws according to house rules.
  5. Settlement: Payouts are made per hand according to the tables below.

Main bet - payouts

Payouts

  • Blackjack (natural 21 in 2 cards) - 3:2
  • Regular win (non-BJ) - 1:1
  • Push - bet returned
  • Lose / Bust - bet lost

All monetary settlements are calculated in whole dollars (fractions round down).

Actions

Double

  • Increase your main bet by the same amount.
  • Receive exactly one additional card (no further hits on that hand).

Surrender

  • Late surrender: reclaim ½ of your main bet and forfeit the hand.
  • Settles immediately; amount rounded down to whole dollars.

Insurance & Even Money

  • Insurance (dealer shows Ace): side stake equals ½ of your main bet (rounded down); pays 2:1 only if the dealer has Blackjack.
  • Even Money (you have Blackjack vs dealer Ace): take an immediate 1:1 payout; the hand ends.

Split

  • Available with two cards of the same rank; creates two hands and requires an additional bet equal to the main bet.
  • Aces: each split Ace receives one card only; no further hits on those hands.
  • No re-splits: you cannot split again after an initial split, even if a split hand becomes a pair.
  • 21 achieved on a split hand is not Blackjack; paid as a regular 21.

Side bets - exact payouts

PERFECT PAIRS - PAYS

  • Perfect Pair (same rank & same suit) - 25 to 1
  • Colored Pair (same rank & same color: ♥♦ or ♣♠) - 12 to 1
  • Mixed Pair (same rank, different colors) - 5 to 1

Evaluated on your first two cards only.

ROYAL MATCH - PAYS

  • Royal Match (King + Queen suited) - 25 to 1
  • Any Suited (any two suited cards) - 5 to 1

Evaluated on your first two cards only.

21 + 3 - PAYS

(Your first two cards + dealer’s up-card)

  • Suited Trips - 100 to 1
  • Straight Flush - 40 to 1
  • Three of a Kind - 30 to 1
  • Straight - 10 to 1
  • Flush - 5 to 1

Ace may count as 1 or 14 for straights.

SUPER SEVENS - PAYS

(Counts your cards plus the dealer’s up-card)

  • One 7 - 3 to 1
  • Two 7s (unsuited) - 50 to 1
  • Two 7s (suited) - 100 to 1
  • Three+ 7s (unsuited) - 500 to 1
  • Three+ 7s (all same suit) - 5000 to 1

Side-bet limits & timing

  • Side bets are available only with a main bet.
  • Max main bet $1000; max per side bet $100 (per seat, per type).
  • All side bets settle immediately after the initial deal.
