BLACKJACK — SINGLE HAND

Beat the dealer by getting closer to 21 without going over.

Payouts

Blackjack (Ace + 10-value on first two cards)pays 3 : 2
Winning handpays 1 : 1
Insurance (offered when dealer shows an Ace)pays 2 : 1
Push (tie with dealer)bet returned
Surrender (forfeit hand)half bet returned

Dealer Rules

  • Dealer must draw to 16 and stand on all 17 (including soft 17).
  • Dealer's second card stays face-down until it is your turn to reveal.
  • Played with a 6-deck shoe, shuffled every hand.

Your Options

  • Hit — take another card.
  • Stand — keep your current hand and end your turn.
  • Double — double your bet and take exactly one more card.
  • Split — with a matching pair, split into two hands.
  • Surrender — give up the hand and get half your bet back.
  • Insurance — side bet offered when the dealer shows an Ace.

Card Values

  • Number cards (2–10) are worth their face value.
  • Jack, Queen, King are each worth 10.
  • Ace is worth 11, or 1 if 11 would bust your hand.
